1. Data Collection

Trickster Companion is designed with a privacy-first, stateless architecture. When you upload or paste a report.json file, the data is processed in-memory to render your view and is not stored in our databases by default.

Our desktop core application remains ad-free and offline-first. This web viewer is a separate companion service intended for report visualization only.
