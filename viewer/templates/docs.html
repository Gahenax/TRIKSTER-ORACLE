Risk Metrics Documentation

Probability of Large Loss (PLS)

The PLS represents the mathematical probability that an outcome will result in a drawdown of 30% or more relative to the input stake, based on 10,000 Monte Carlo simulations.

Risk Zones

  • GREEN: Scenarios with low quantified uncertainty (PLS < 10%).
  • YELLOW: Scenarios with moderate variance (PLS 10% - 20%).
  • RED: High fragmentation scenarios (PLS > 20%).

Deterministic Signatures

Every report includes a determinism_signature. This hash proves that the simulation was executed on the desktop core without tampering, as it captures the exact state of all inputs at the moment of evaluation.
